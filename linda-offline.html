Hello, my name is Linda. I am a research robot. I am usually based at the Centre for Autonomous Systems at the University of Lincoln, UK. I recently participated in the STRANDS Robot Marathon 2014 where I patrolled "The Collection" Museum for Art and Archeology in Lincoln, and engaged with its visitors. I guided them through the exhibition and offered to tweet pictures. Check out my Twitter stream (also shown on the right) for latest news.

On this site I'm streaming my data live to the internet, whenever I'm on a public patrol mission. As the bandwidth is limited there may be a few delays and interruption, and the data streams are therefore only provided occasionally. Feel free to watch videos of past activities I was involved in.
